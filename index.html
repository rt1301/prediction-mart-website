What Is a Prediction Market?

The essentials—kept simple and visual.

1) Event Definition

An event with uncertain outcomes is defined. Each outcome gets a contract (often binary: Yes/No).

  • “Will Kamala Harris win the 2024 U.S. Presidential Election?”
  • “Will Apple release an AI-powered iPhone by June 2025?”
  • “Will global temperatures exceed 1.5°C above pre-industrial levels in 2024?”

2) Trading Contracts

Buy/sell shares tied to outcomes:

  • “Yes” pays $1 if the event happens, $0 if not.
  • “No” pays $1 if it doesn’t happen, $0 if it does.
  • Prices range from $0.00–$1.00, interpreted as probabilities.
  • Example: “SpaceX lands Starship on Mars by 2030?” trading at $0.22 ⇒ market believes 22% chance.

3) Price = Probability

Market prices reflect aggregated beliefs:

  • $0.85 ⇒ ~85% chance the event occurs.
  • $0.05 ⇒ ~5% chance (unlikely).

Similar to how stock prices reflect collective expectations of value.

4) Settlement

After the event occurs (or the deadline passes), contracts pay out:

  • “Yes” holders get $1/share if the outcome is true.
  • “No” holders get $1/share if the outcome is false.
  • Profits/losses: (Payout − Purchase Price) × Shares.

How It Works

From onboarding to payout.

  1. Sign up → Create an account.
  2. Deposit funds → Add ₹ or $ balance (jurisdiction permitting).
  3. Pick an event → Browse tech, trade, economics, geopolitics.
  4. Buy shares → Choose Yes or No based on your view.
  5. Trade or hold → Sell before resolution or hold to settlement.
  6. Settle → Correct side pays $1/share; profits are credited.

Example Transactions

Quick, numerical walkthroughs.

Example A — Buy “Yes” and Hold

Inputs: Price = $0.40, Investment = $100

Shares PurchasedInvestment / Price = 100 / 0.40 = 250
Max Payout if “Yes”250 × $1.00 = $250.00
Profit if Correct (pre-fees)$250 − $100 = $150.00
Loss if Incorrect (pre-fees)$0 − $100 = −$100.00

Example B — Sell Before Settlement

Inputs: Buy “Yes” at $0.60 with $100; later price = $0.75

Shares Purchased100 / 0.60 = 166.67
Proceeds at Sale166.67 × 0.75 = $125.00
P&L (pre-fees)$125.00 − $100.00 = +$25.00
